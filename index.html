 Why should you use Duino-Coin

  1. It's the only coin that can be mined on Arduino and ESP8266 boards and additionally with smartphones, computers and single-board computers
  2. It does many things differently than other cryptos
  3. It's being developed by a growing group of (mostly) young crypto, electonics and IT enthusiasts
  4. It's a completely original creation (isn't just a lazy fork)
  5. It has instant transactions (you don't have to wait half an hour to transfer funds)
  6. It's completely open source and in constant development
  7. It wants to achieve literal low-power mining with fair rewards
  8. It's price may go up in the future

Why not give it a try when it's all free, you will learn something new and make a bit of money in the process thanks to already existing exchange sites?

 What is the point of mining on AVR boards

Simply saying - because now you can. There is literally NO other cryptocurrency that supports it.
Many people have Arduinos that are just laying around waiting for better days - we thought it'd be a cool idea to put these small devices in use in the crypto world, especially since they're low-power.
We wanted to create something new, innovative and unique, and from the size of community we've gathered, it looks like we made ultra low-energy devices mining possible.

If the idea of setting it all up sounds hard for you - don't worry. We have some nice guides to make it all easy.
